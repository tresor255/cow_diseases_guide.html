Common Cow Diseases

Mastitis

Symptoms: Swollen udder, pain, abnormal milk.

Treatment: Antibiotics and hygiene management.

Foot and Mouth Disease (FMD)

Symptoms: Blisters, fever, lameness.

Treatment: Vaccination and isolation of infected animals.

Johne’s Disease

Symptoms: Chronic diarrhea, weight loss despite a good appetite, decreased milk production.

Treatment: No effective treatment; control through testing, culling, and improving sanitation practices.

Lumpy Skin Disease

Symptoms: Diarrhea, dehydration, weakness, weight loss.

Treatment: No effective treatment once symptoms show; prevention through vaccination and quick culling of infected animals..

Blackleg

Symptoms: Swelling, fever, lameness, muscle tissue death.

Treatment: No effective treatment; control through testing, culling, and improving sanitation practices.

Bloat

Symptoms:Sudden death, bleeding from orifices, high fever, swelling..

Treatment: Immediate isolation of affected animals, vaccination, and antibiotic treatment if detected early..

Brucellosis

Symptoms:Distended left side of the abdomen, difficulty breathing, discomfort..

Treatment: Oral anti-foaming agents, stomach tube, trocharization in severe cases, and dietary adjustments..

Anthrax

Symptoms:Sudden death, bleeding from orifices, high fever, swelling...

Treatment: Oral anti-foaming agents, stomach tube, trocharization in severe cases, and dietary adjustments..
