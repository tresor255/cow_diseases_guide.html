Common Cow Diseases

Mastitis

Symptoms: Swollen udder, pain, abnormal milk.

Treatment: Use antibiotics and ensure hygiene.

Foot and Mouth Disease (FMD)

Symptoms: Blisters, fever, lameness.

Treatment: Vaccinate and isolate infected animals.
